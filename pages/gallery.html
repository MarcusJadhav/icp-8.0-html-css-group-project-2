Fruits and Vegetables

Discover a vibrant selection of fresh, nutritious fruits and vegetables.
Perfect for enhancing your meals with natural flavors, essential vitamins, and minerals, our produce ensures a wholesome addition to your diet.

Strawberry

Strawberries are vibrant, heart-shaped red fruits with a sweet, juicy flavor and tiny seeds on their surface. They are rich in vitamin C, antioxidants, and dietary fiber. Popular in desserts, jams, and snacks, they are both delicious and visually appealing.

Citrus Fruits

Citrus fruits are tangy, juicy fruits with a bright, zesty flavor, including oranges, lemons, limes, and grapefruits. They are rich in vitamin C, antioxidants, and fiber. Known for their vibrant colors and refreshing taste, they are widely used in beverages, desserts, and savory dishes.

Kiwi

Kiwis are small, brown, fuzzy fruits with a vibrant green interior and tiny black seeds. They have a sweet-tart flavor and are rich in vitamin C, vitamin K, and fiber. Often enjoyed fresh, in salads, or as a garnish, kiwis are both delicious and nutritious.

Mix Salad

A fruit mix salad is a refreshing and colorful dish made by combining a variety of fresh fruits such as strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, oranges, and grapes. Often enhanced with a drizzle of honey or a squeeze of lemon juice, it offers a delightful blend of sweet and tangy flavors. This vibrant salad is not only visually appealing but also packed with vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, making it a healthy and delicious choice for any meal.

Dairy and Eggs

Enjoy our selection of fresh dairy products and high-quality eggs.
Perfect for adding richness and essential nutrients to your meals, our dairy and eggs are staples for a balanced and delicious diet.

Milk

Milk is a nutrient-rich liquid produced by mammals, commonly consumed by humans. It is an excellent source of calcium, protein, and vitamins D and B12. Milk is versatile, used in beverages, cooking, and baking.

Butter-Milk

Buttermilk is a tangy, fermented dairy product, traditionally the liquid left after churning butter from cream. It's rich in probiotics, low in fat, and often used in baking, cooking, and as a refreshing drink. Its unique flavor and acidity make it ideal for tenderizing and adding moisture to recipes.

Eggs

Eggs are versatile, nutrient-dense foods, high in protein, vitamins, and minerals. They are used in a variety of dishes, from breakfast staples to baking and cooking. Eggs can be prepared in numerous ways, including boiling, frying, scrambling, and poaching.

Cheese

Cheese is a delicious dairy product made from curdled milk, with a wide range of flavors, textures, and varieties. It's rich in calcium, protein, and essential nutrients like vitamin B12. Used in cooking, snacking, and as a topping, cheese adds creamy richness and depth to dishes.

Meat and Seafood

Explore our range of premium meats and fresh seafood.
Perfect for creating hearty, protein-rich meals, our selection ensures you have the best quality ingredients for your culinary creations.

Meat

Meat, rich in proteins and essential nutrients,
offers diverse flavors and textures, enhancing culinary experiences globally through various cooking methods.

Prawns

Prawns are shellfish with a sweet, delicate flavor, prized for their tender texture and versatility in cooking. They are rich in protein, low in fat, and a popular choice in various cuisines, from stir-fries to seafood pastas.

Fish

Fish are aquatic animals consumed as a source of protein and essential nutrients like omega-3 fatty acids. They offer diverse flavors and textures, making them a versatile ingredient in cuisines worldwide, from grilled salmon to sushi delicacies.

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish, originating from the Indian subcontinent, typically made with basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables. It's cooked using a variety of techniques, such as layering and slow cooking, resulting in a rich and complex flavor profile loved by many.

Bakery and Bread

Indulge in our assortment of freshly baked goods and artisanal breads.
Perfect for any meal or snack, our bakery items offer delightful flavors and textures to satisfy your cravings.

Garlic Bread

Garlic bread is a savory treat made with bread, typically baguette or ciabatta, infused with garlic butter and herbs. It's often toasted or baked until golden brown, resulting in a crispy exterior and soft, flavorful interior. A popular side dish or appetizer, garlic bread complements pasta dishes and soups deliciously.

Slice Cake

A slice of cake is a delightful indulgence, featuring a moist and flavorful sponge cake layered with creamy frosting or filling. It's a sweet treat enjoyed on various occasions, from birthdays to celebrations, offering a burst of flavor and decadence in every bite.

Cookies

Cookies are delightful baked treats made from dough consisting of flour, sugar, butter, and other ingredients like chocolate chips or nuts. They come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors, offering a satisfyingly sweet and crunchy texture. Perfect for snacking or dessert, cookies are beloved worldwide for their versatility and deliciousness.

Pancake

Pancakes are fluffy, round breakfast delights made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder. They're cooked on a griddle or skillet until golden brown and served hot with toppings like syrup, fruit, or whipped cream. Loved for their soft texture and versatility, pancakes are a comforting morning favorite enjoyed by many.

Snacks

Satisfy your cravings with our selection of tasty snacks.
From savory to sweet, our variety of snacks is perfect for any time of day, providing delightful flavors and convenience.

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish consisting of a round, flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients like meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbling, pizza offers a delicious combination of flavors and textures. A popular choice for gatherings or quick meals, pizza satisfies cravings with its savory and customizable appeal.

Red Sauce Pasta

Red sauce pasta is a flavorful dish made with pasta coated in a tomato-based sauce infused with garlic, onions, herbs, and sometimes chili flakes for a hint of spice. It's a classic Italian-inspired comfort food, often served with grated cheese and fresh basil for added flavor. Enjoyed for its rich and tangy taste, red sauce pasta is a satisfying meal suitable for any occasion.

Yougurt Salad

Green peas can add a burst of freshness and sweetness to yogurt salad. Incorporating blanched or thawed green peas into the salad enhances its texture and adds vibrant color. Combined with diced cucumbers, mint leaves, and a squeeze of lemon juice, this yogurt salad with green peas offers a delightful balance of flavors and nutrients.

Noodles

Noodles are versatile strands of dough made from flour, water, and sometimes eggs, commonly used in various cuisines worldwide. They come in different shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, fettuccine, or ramen, and can be served in soups, stir-fries, or salads. With their satisfying texture and ability to absorb flavors, noodles are a staple ingredient in many delicious dishes.

Sweets

Delight in our selection of delectable sweets. From chocolates to candies, our range of sweet treats offers a perfect indulgence for any occasion.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a popular Indian dessert made from milk solids, traditionally kneaded into a dough, shaped into balls, deep-fried until golden brown, and then soaked in sugar syrup flavored with rose water, saffron, and cardamom. These soft, spongy balls are a favorite at celebrations and festivals, offering a decadent, sweet treat with aromatic hints of spice.

Jalebi

Jalebi is a classic Indian sweet made from a fermented batter of flour, yogurt, and saffron, piped into pretzel or circular shapes, deep-fried until crispy, and then soaked in a sugar syrup infused with cardamom and saffron. These golden, syrupy spirals are a beloved street food and festive treat, known for their crunchy texture and indulgent sweetness.

Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a popular Indian dessert consisting of soft, flattened dumplings made from paneer (Indian cottage cheese), soaked in a creamy, sweetened milk syrup flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts. It's chilled and served cold, garnished with additional nuts such as pistachios or almonds. Rasmalai is loved for its delicate texture and rich, aromatic taste, making it a favorite indulgence during special occasions and celebrations.

Choclates

Chocolate is a beloved treat made from cocoa solids, cocoa butter, and sugar, offering a rich and indulgent flavor loved worldwide. Available in various forms such as bars, truffles, and candies, chocolate comes in a range of flavors and intensities to suit every palate. Whether enjoyed on its own or as an ingredient in desserts, chocolate delights the senses with its smooth texture and decadent taste.

Bevarages

Quench your thirst with our variety of refreshing beverages. From juices to soft drinks, our selection offers something for everyone to enjoy.

Blue Ocean

"Blue Ocean" is a refreshing beverage known for its vibrant blue color and tropical flavor profile. Typically, it's a blend of citrus juices like lemon or lime, mixed with blue curaçao liqueur and sometimes coconut cream or pineapple juice. The result is a sweet and tangy drink reminiscent of a tropical paradise, often served over ice and garnished with a slice of citrus or a maraschino cherry.

Blue Lagoon

The Blue Lagoon is a popular cocktail known for its striking blue color and tropical taste. It typically contains vodka, blue curaçao, and lemonade or citrus soda, creating a refreshing and visually appealing drink reminiscent of a clear tropical lagoon. Garnished with a lemon slice or a maraschino cherry, the Blue Lagoon is a favorite at parties and beach bars for its vibrant hue and delightful flavor.

Dragon Fruit Juice

Dragon fruit juice is a refreshing beverage made from the pulp of ripe dragon fruit, also known as pitaya. It's prized for its vibrant pink color and mildly sweet, tropical flavor with hints of kiwi and pear. Often served chilled and sometimes mixed with other fruits or coconut water for added sweetness and hydration, dragon fruit juice is a popular choice for its refreshing taste and vibrant appearance.

Latte

A latte is a popular coffee beverage made with espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a small amount of milk foam. It has a creamy texture and a mild coffee flavor, making it a favorite choice for those who prefer a smoother, less intense coffee experience. Lattes can be customized with various syrups or flavorings, such as vanilla or caramel, to suit individual preferences.
