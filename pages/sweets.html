Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a sweet cake made with chocolate or cocoa powder, and can also include other ingredients like vanilla creme, fudge, and sweeteners.

Ice Cake

An iced cake is a cake with an icing made of sugar, usually powdered, and a liquid or a fat. An ice box cake is a no bake cake. Usually made of layers of mousse and crushed cookies.

Pineapple Cake

Pineapple cake is a traditional Taiwanese dessert that's usually square or rectangular in shape and has a shortbread-like crust and a sweet and tangy pineapple filling.

Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cake is a popular, soft, and velvety-textured cake from the Southern United States.
