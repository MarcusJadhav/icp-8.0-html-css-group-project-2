ABOUT US - OUR JOURNEY


Welcome to DELUX!

At Delux, we're passionate about food, flavor, and bringing people together through the joy of cooking. Our journey began in the bustling streets of Mumbai, where the aroma of spices and the sizzle of street food inspired us to create a platform dedicated to sharing the culinary wonders of this vibrant city.

Our Mission

is simple: to celebrate Mumbai's rich culinary heritage and empower home cooks with delicious recipes, helpful tips, and a supportive community. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, we believe that everyone can find joy and satisfaction in creating mouthwatering dishes right in their own kitchen.

Meet the Chef

Hi, I'm Rachael Ray, the chef behind Delux. With a lifelong passion for cooking and a deep appreciation for Mumbai's diverse flavors, I'm thrilled to share my expertise and recipes with you. From classic street food favorites to modern twists on traditional dishes, each recipe is crafted with love and a touch of Mumbai magic.

Join Our Community

Cooking is more than just following a recipe - it's about sharing experiences, swapping stories, and connecting with others who share your love for food. Join our growing community of food enthusiasts and let's embark on a culinary adventure together. Whether you're looking for cooking advice, recipe inspiration, or just want to share your latest kitchen triumph, you'll find a warm welcome here at Delux.
