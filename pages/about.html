Welcome to DELUX!

At Delux, we're passionate about food, flavor, and bringing people together through the joy of cooking. Our journey began in the bustling streets of Mumbai, where the aroma of spices and the sizzle of street food inspired us to create a platform dedicated to sharing the culinary wonders of this vibrant city.I'm thrilled to share my culinary journey with you. Our store in Mumbai is more than just a place to find delicious recipes; it's a hub of flavors and traditions. From traditional Mumbai street food to modern fusion creations, we aim to bring a taste of Mumbai's vibrant food scene to your kitchen. Join me as we explore the rich tapestry of flavors that make Mumbai a culinary paradise. Let's cook up something special together!

Our Mission

to celebrate Mumbai's rich culinary heritage and empower home cooks with delicious recipes, helpful tips, and a supportive community. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, we believe that everyone can find joy and satisfaction in creating mouthwatering dishes right in their own kitchen.
